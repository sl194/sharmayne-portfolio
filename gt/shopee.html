Project Description

This was the second stage of the Shopee UI/UX internship application, where I was invited to complete a remote UI /UX Design Test.

Test Topic: Redesigning Shopee Home Tab
Description: The Home tab is the first touch point of an e-commerce platform, making it a very important feature as it impacts GMV directly. Currently, the click rate performance of each module is assumed to not be good enough.

As a UI/UX designer, please redesign this page for the 6+1 greater SEA region (Indonesia, Taiwan, Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore, Philippines) to improve the performance of the overall click rate based on your experience and the limited information we have provided to you.
