Project Description

After extending my internship at Grab, I had the rare opportunity to participate in Grab's annual internal hackathon -- Grabathon 9.0! After about 48 hours of hacking, we emerged victorious as 3rd among 422 participants across 70 teams with 160 problem statements in total, under the Golden Faithfuls (Loyalty initiatives) category. Check out this article to find out more about the experience!


THE GRABPETS TEAM


Day 0

A day before the hackathon, I put together a one-pager of our problem statement, data and research about existing experiments we've implemented to improve user loyalty, as well as a brief analysis of competitors which have used gamification strategies with mascots or characters to engage and retain their users.

Day 1

Early in the morning, after settling our daily stand-ups and urgent tasks, we quickly met to run through what I had put together to get everyone on the same page. Following that, we ideated for about 30 minutes on FigJam, then Andrew and I worked together to categorize the various ideas before deciding on a few to develop further.
