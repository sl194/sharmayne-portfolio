Project Brief

In January 2020, as part of SUTD Rotaract's overseas community involvement programme, we worked with ECO Vietnam Group to help a Vietnamese village in Tra Vinh. We built a water system, constructed a road and taught students STEM subjects there. Not only that, we met new friends who were local university volunteers and exchanged cultures. We fished with our hands and had those fish for our BBQ dinner, went to the farm to harvest coconuts, visited an ant farm and many more adventures! It was truly an eye-opening experience for us, truly one of a kind.
